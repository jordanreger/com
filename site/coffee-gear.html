a note on the aeropress
the aeropress is a sacred piece of coffee technology. ask literally any aeropress user how they feel about drip coffee, pourovers and god forbid drip coffee. there is nothing greater than a cup of practically any type of coffee pressed from an aeropress. i use my aeropress to determine how good a coffee is - hence, the list here.
