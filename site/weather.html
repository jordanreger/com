weather
i am a NWS SKYWARN certified storm spotter. 22 was my first year spotting. it didn't yield anything historic, but still proved to be interesting as i was learning about storm development and how to report it to the NWS.

this page contains a log of notable meteorological events that i've covered (or just... noted).


22
  • 22S13 — hurricane ian
    hurricane ian formed 22S13 (september 23) and made landfall as an extremely strong category 4 hurricane near punta gorda, florida on 22T04.

  • 22I06 — andover tornado
    the main event of this system was the andover EF-3 tornado. i actually have a friend who lived there who alerted me of this storm about 5 minutes after the main tornado had passed through. reed timmer's video captures some of the most interesting footage i've ever seen of multiple vortices.
