coffee
coffee is a big part of my life. i drink it practically everyday, but not because i am caffeine-dependent (i promise, i broke that a while ago), but because i love the taste. i can drink it practically any way: black, with cream, sugar, with oatmilk, you name it - i love it. i prefer to make my own or let my partner do it, because she's also a coffee fan; very rarely do i get coffee from coffee shops.


my coffee gear
  • aeropress
  • proctor silex electric water kettle
  • le creuset mug (2x)
  • a handheld grinder (i have no idea what brand it is)


a note on the aeropress
the aeropress is a sacred piece of coffee technology. ask literally any aeropress user how they feel about drip coffee, pourovers and god forbid drip coffee. there is nothing greater than a cup of practically any type of coffee pressed from an aeropress. i use my aeropress to determine how good a coffee is - hence, the list below.
